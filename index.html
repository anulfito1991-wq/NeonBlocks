NEONBLOCKS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

NeonBlocks is an 8×8 block puzzle game developed by Anulfo Acosta. This policy describes how the app handles data. We've tried to keep it short because there isn't much to say — the app collects nothing and sends nothing to a server we operate.

What data we collect

We collect no personal data. NeonBlocks does not create accounts, require sign-in, request your email, or send any information to a server operated by us. The app has no backend.

Local-only data

All game state — your high score, coin balance, settings, achievement progress, daily streak, lifetime statistics — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. This data never leaves the device. Deleting the app removes all of it.

Game Center

If NeonBlocks has Game Center enabled (check the in-app Achievements screen for a Game Center button), and you are signed into Game Center on your device, your Game Center alias and leaderboard score may be shared with Apple's Game Center service. This is governed by Apple's Game Center terms, not by us. You can disable Game Center in your device's iOS Settings at any time.

In-app purchases

If NeonBlocks offers in-app purchases, they are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see your payment information. Apple provides us with anonymous transaction receipts so we can unlock the content you bought.

Advertising

NeonBlocks v1 does not show ads and does not link any advertising SDK. If a future version introduces ads, this policy will be updated to disclose which SDK is used and what data it collects.

Analytics and tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track you across apps or websites. The App Store itself provides aggregate, anonymous install and session counts to us through Apple's App Store Connect dashboard; this data is provided by Apple under their terms and is not individually identifiable.

Children's privacy

NeonBlocks is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13.

Notifications

NeonBlocks can send you local reminders about the daily challenge and your play streak, but only if you opt in from the Settings screen. These notifications are scheduled on your device by iOS itself — no server is involved, and no notification data is sent anywhere. You can disable them at any time in the app's Settings or in iOS Settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will appear at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
